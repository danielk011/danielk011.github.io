About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Texas at Austin in Mathematics. I did my undergraduate degree at the University of Utah. On a lazy Saturday, I would enjoy hiking, cooking, climbing, and running. Recently, you may have encountered me at the summer school on Enumerative Geometry and Geometric Representation Theory or the SRI in Algebraic Geometry at CSU Fort Collins. I have published research on proving a combinatorial Lefschetz type property for simplicial cycles by proving p-anisotropy. My advisor is Bernd Siebert

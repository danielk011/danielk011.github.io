Cech Cohomology

A formal project I have been doing over the last few years is an expository project on Cech Cohomology. It began as an introductory project on Riemann surfaces. Afterwards, I was introduced sheaves during a Directed Reading Project. The project culminated in the second half of this expository paper. While the project wasn't necessarily very deep, I hope to do more interesting things with cohomology with category theory that I develop from Modern Algebra this year.
